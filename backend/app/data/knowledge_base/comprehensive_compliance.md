## AICTE COMPLIANCE

### 1. Extension of Approval (EoA) — Annual
- **Form:** EoA-APH-2024-27 | **Portal:** approvals.aicte-india.org
- **Deadline:** Nov 6–20 filing; Apr–Jun for EoA letter
- **Data Required:** Faculty count + SFR (≤1:20 for B.Tech), cadre ratio 1:2:6, intake per program, land area (acres), built-up area (sqm), NBA/NAAC status, fee structure, audited financials (3 years)
- **Key Rules:** Faculty ID must be Aadhaar-PAN linked. Late fee ₹1L. No EoA = no admissions.
- **SFR Formula:** Total students / Total regular faculty ≤ 1:20 per program
- **Cadre Ratio:** Professor : Associate Professor : Assistant Professor = 1:2:6

### 2. Mandatory Disclosure — Annual + Continuous
- **Form:** APH-2024-27 Annexure-18 | 21 sections
- **Key Sections:** Institution ID, trust/society, BoG details, programs (intake + NBA + NAAC + 3yr cutoffs), faculty (each: PAN + designation + qualification + experience), fee structure, 3-yr placement (company + CTC), grants + patents, audited A/c, anti-ragging committee
- **Rules:** Must be on institution website, searchable without login, updated within 7 days of any change
- **Non-compliance:** Approval withdrawal; penalty up to 2× fee per student

### 3. Faculty Disclosure (Faculty ID Module) — Continuous
- **Portal:** AICTE Faculty Portal (approvals.aicte-india.org)
- **Data:** Faculty ID (PAN + Aadhaar linked), name, designation, department, qualification (UG/PG/PhD), date of join/exit, experience (industry + teaching), GATE/NET qualification, publications
- **Key Rule:** Faculty not in portal = doesn't count for SFR or cadre ratio. Every join/exit must be updated same day.

### 4. Anti-Ragging Compliance — Annual
- **Portal:** antiragging.in | **Deadline:** 31 Jul (existing); 15 Aug (freshers)
- **Data:** Affidavit % submitted (student + parent, target 100%), Committee composition (reconstituted annually), Squad members, incidents log, helpline 1800-180-5522 display status
- **Non-compliance:** Approval withdrawal; criminal liability on Principal

### 5. APAAR / ABC Credit Upload — Semester-wise
- **Portal:** abc.gov.in | **Deadline:** Each semester-end before exam results
- **Data:** Student APAAR ID, programme, semester, subject codes, credits earned, CGPA, pass/fail
- **Rule:** B.Tech = 160 credits total (NHEQF Level 6). Students without APAAR ID cannot graduate.

### 6. Internship Compliance — Continuous
- **Portal:** internship.aicte-india.org
- **Credits:** 4-week after 2nd sem (2 credits), 6-week after 4th (3 credits), 6-month in 7th sem (12–15 credits) = 14–20 total credits
- **Data:** Company, duration, stipend, type (industry/research/NGO), mentor, credits earned, certificate, employer feedback

## NBA COMPLIANCE (GAPC v4.0 — 2025)

### Pre-Qualifier (Gateway — must all pass before SAR)
- **SFR:** ≤ 1:25 (3-year average of CAY/m1/m2)
- **Admission ratio:** ≥ 60% (3-year average)
- **PhD faculty:** ≥ 20%
- **HOD:** Must have PhD in parent discipline
- **Professor/Associate Professor:** Min 2 each with PhD per department
- **Graduated batches:** Min 2 batches must have graduated
- **Non-compliance:** Cannot file SAR; ₹1L registration fee forfeited

### SAR Criterion 1: Outcome-Based Curriculum (120 marks)
- PEOs (3–5, stakeholder-derived, reviewed every 2 years): 40 marks
- CO-PO-WK mapping matrix (11 POs per GAPC v4.0): 30 marks
- Curriculum plan with Bloom's taxonomy: 15 marks
- SWAYAM/NPTEL credits (min 20% of electives): 10 marks

### SAR Criterion 2: Teaching-Learning (120 marks)
- Capstone project (final 2-semester, industry problem statements): 25 marks
- Innovative pedagogy (flipped classroom, PBL, experiential): evidence required
- NPTEL completion certificates required
- Lab-to-theory ratio documented

### SAR Criterion 3: Assessment — CO-PO Attainment (120 marks)
**CO Attainment Formula:**
```
CO_direct = (CIE_marks / max_CIE) × 0.40 + (SEE_marks / max_SEE) × 0.60
CO_indirect = exit_survey_score  (alumni/employer survey)
CO_attainment = (CO_direct × 0.80) + (CO_indirect × 0.20)
Attained = CO_attainment >= 60%  (default threshold; editable per program)

PO_attainment = Σ(CO_attainment_i × correlation_i) / Σ(correlation_i)
correlation: 1=Slight, 2=Moderate, 3=Substantial
```
- CO attainment (25 marks), PO/PSO attainment (25 marks)
- Gap analysis trigger: CO < 60% → mandatory action plan next semester
- SDG portfolio mapping (new in GAPC v4.0 Criterion 3.6)
- **Non-compliance:** Criterion fail (<60%); 6-yr accreditation not possible

### SAR Criterion 4: Student Performance (120 marks)
- **APY Formula:** (students who passed in N years / admitted in that batch) × 100
- **Placement %:** placed / eligible (NOT placed / total — common error)
- **Offer letters mandatory** evidence for placement data
- GATE qualifiers = PO11 attainment proxy
- Enrolment ratio (20 marks), Success rate 3yr APY (30 marks), Placement (30 marks), Professional activities (25 marks)

### SAR Criteria 5 & 6: Faculty (220 marks total)
- C5 (100 marks): SFR target 1:15 for excellence, qualifications, cadre ratio, retention %
- C6 (120 marks): Scopus/WoS publications only (CARE list discontinued Oct 2024), citations, h-index, funded grants (₹ value), patents (filed/granted), consultancy revenue
- **PhD verification:** Must be Scopus-verifiable thesis

### SAR Criteria 7, 8, 9: Facilities/Improvement/Governance (300 marks total)
- C7 (100): Lab area ≥ 2 sqm/student, equipment inventory, safety certificates (fire + CEA)
- C8 (80): Annual program review minutes, alumni/employer survey actions (≥20% response rate), action-taken report
- C9 (120): Scholarships, grievance resolution %, anti-ragging status, governance structure

### Post-Accreditation Compliance Report (PACR)
- File 6 months before accreditation expiry (not 3 months)
- Triggers 2-member surveillance visit
- Non-filing = automatic expiry

## NAAC COMPLIANCE (Binary Accreditation — 2024 Reform)

### IIQA (Gateway Application)
- Fee: ₹2L + 18% GST
- Prerequisites: AICTE EoA + AISHE code mandatory
- Submit 3+ months before SSR target
- 6 mandatory documents: AICTE EoA, AISHE cert, affiliation cert, fee structure, anti-ragging cert, faculty list

### SSR (Self-Study Report) — Engineering Weightage
- Total: 1000 points
- Criterion 1 Curricular: 100
- **Criterion 2 TLE: 350 (HEAVIEST for engineering)**
- Criterion 3 Research: 150
- Criterion 4 Infrastructure: 100
- Criterion 5 Student Support: 130
- Criterion 6 Governance: 100
- Criterion 7 Values: 100
- 68 quantitative metrics (auto-populated from ERP/LMS) + 41 qualitative narratives (AI-draftable)
- Under Binary 2024: 25% input + 75% process-output
- Data MUST match AISHE + NIRF exactly (zero tolerance on faculty count)

### AQAR (Annual Quality Assurance Report) — **CRITICAL Dec 31 Deadline**
- **DECEMBER 31 = EXISTENTIAL deadline. Missing it BLOCKS ENTIRE NEXT ACCREDITATION CYCLE.**
- Start data collection November
- Part A: IQAC composition + meetings + innovations
- Part B: 7 criteria (1-year scope)
- Action Taken Report: previous recommendations → specific action → evidence URL → status
- Best Practices 1 + 2 (AI-draftable)
- Cross-verify with AISHE/NIRF of same year

### DVV Response
- 7–15 day window
- Engineering-specific checks: faculty count, placement %, research publications, campus area, NBA status
- Pre-flag system: cross-check AICTE vs NAAC vs NIRF vs AISHE weekly (zero tolerance on faculty count)

## NIRF (Annual — March 16 Deadline)

### 5 Parameters and Sub-metrics
**TLR (30%):**
- SS: Student strength including PhD
- FSR: Faculty-student ratio (target 1:15 for top score)
- FQE: Faculty with PhD
- FRU: Financial resources per student (expenditure/student ₹)

**RPC (30%):**
- PU: Scopus/WoS publications per faculty (3-year)
- QP: Quality publications (citations)
- IPR: Patents (15 marks — high impact)
- FPPP: Professional practice/consultancy (10 marks)

**GO (20%):**
- GPH: Placement % (placed/eligible)
- GUE: Higher studies %
- GMS: Median CTC
- GPHD: PhD graduates

**OI (10%):**
- RD: Regional diversity
- WD: Women diversity (women students + women faculty %)
- ESCS: Economically/socially challenged students (SC/ST/OBC/PwD %)
- PCS: Facilities for differently abled

**Perception (10%):** Academic peer survey

**Rules:** Faculty counts MUST match AICTE + NAAC + AISHE (zero tolerance). Negative marking for retracted publications (2025). Placement % = placed/eligible (not total).

## AISHE DCF-II (Annual — October 15)

### 12 Blocks
1. Basic info (AISHE code, institution type)
2. Programmes (discipline code 7 for engineering)
3. Teaching staff (designation × gender × category: SC/ST/OBC/Gen/EWS)
4. Non-teaching staff
5. Enrollment (programme × year × gender × category)
6. Results (pass % by programme)
7. Infrastructure (campus area + lab area)
8. Scholarships (scheme-wise count + amount)
9. Financials (income + expenditure)
10. NEP adoption status
11. NBA/NAAC status (programme-wise)
12. Placement data

**Critical:** Reference date for staff = 31 December. Teaching staff MUST match AICTE + NAAC + NIRF exactly.

## UGC COMPLIANCE

### Self-Disclosure — 12 Categories (Annual + Continuous)
- Must be on website, searchable without login, updated within 7 days
- Categories: programmes + fees, faculty vacancies, infrastructure, anti-ragging, ICC (POSH), SGRC, grievances, scholarships, NEP adoption, NIRF participation
- 54 universities penalised September 2025 for non-compliance

### POSH ICC Annual Report — January 31
- **To:** District Officer (under Rule 14 POSH Rules 2013)
- ICC composition: minimum 8 members, ≥50% women, Presiding Officer = senior woman faculty
- Tenure: 3 years (not co-terminus with admin appointment)
- Inquiry completion: 90-day limit from complaint date
- Penalty: ₹50,000 first offence; licence cancellation on repeat

### SGRC (Grievance Redressal) — Continuous
- UGC Regulations 2023 (Gazette 11 Apr 2023)
- Ombudsperson (university level) required
- 15 working-day resolution mandatory
- Portal: saksham.ugc.ac.in

## LABOUR COMPLIANCE

### EPF + ESIC Monthly — 15th of Every Month (CRITICAL)
- **EPF:** 12% employee + 12% employer (3.67% EPF + 8.33% EPS + 0.50% EDLI + 0.50% admin)
- **ESIC:** 0.75% employee + 3.25% employer
- **Code on Wages (Nov 2025):** Basic salary ≥ 50% of CTC
- **Portal:** epfindia.gov.in and esic.gov.in
- **Late payment:** 12% p.a. interest + 5–25% damages under Section 14B + criminal liability
- Monthly ECR (Electronic Challan-cum-Return) must be filed

### POSH Annual Report — January 31 (same as ICC above)

## KEY FORMULAS AND THRESHOLDS

```
# NBA OBE Attainment
CO_direct = (CIE/max_CIE) × 0.40 + (SEE/max_SEE) × 0.60
CO_attainment = CO_direct × 0.80 + CO_indirect × 0.20
Attained if CO_attainment >= 0.60 (60%)
PO = Σ(CO_att × corr) / Σ(corr)  [corr: 1=slight, 2=moderate, 3=substantial]

# NBA APY (Student Performance)
APY1 = (passed_in_4yrs / admitted_4yr_ago) × 100
APY2 = (passed_in_5yrs / admitted_5yr_ago) × 100
APY3 = (passed_in_6yrs / admitted_6yr_ago) × 100

# NIRF
Placement % = placed / eligible × 100  [NOT placed/total — common mistake]
FSR = total_students / total_regular_faculty
FQE = faculty_with_phd / total_faculty × 100
FRU = total_expenditure / total_students  [₹ per student]

# AICTE
SFR = total_students / total_regular_faculty  [must be ≤ 1:20 for UG B.Tech]
Cadre ratio = Prof : Assoc Prof : Asst Prof = 1 : 2 : 6
```
